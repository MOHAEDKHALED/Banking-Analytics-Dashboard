## 📋 Project Overview

This project is a **full-stack Business Intelligence (BI) solution** designed to monitor, analyze, and visualize key banking performance metrics. It provides deep insights into customer behavior, financial health, operational efficiency, risk management, and customer support quality.

The dashboard helps bank executives and analysts make **data-driven decisions** regarding customer retention, revenue growth, loan performance, and service improvement.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- **5,000+ Customers** with rich demographic and behavioral data
- **20,000+ Transactions** spanning 2022–2025
- Full **Time Intelligence** (Monthly, Quarterly, Yearly, Seasonal, and Trend Analysis)
- Advanced analytics: **Churn Analysis**, **Cohort Analysis**, **RFM Segmentation**
- Interactive dashboards with drill-through, cross-filtering, and bookmarks
- Seasonal performance analysis (Spring, Summer, Fall, Winter)
- Geo-spatial analysis by U.S. State

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Measure customer acquisition and retention
- Track revenue streams and balance growth
- Monitor loan portfolio health and interest rate trends
- Identify service bottlenecks through support call analysis
- Detect dormant accounts and at-risk customers

---

## 📊 Core KPIs (Heroes)

### Customers
- Total Customers & Growth Trends
- Customer Distribution by State
- Join Date Analysis (Month/Year/Season)
- **Churn Rate** and **Cohort Retention Analysis**

### Accounts
- Total Accounts & Total Balance
- Account Type Breakdown (Savings, Checking, Business)
- Balance Distribution & Average Balance per Customer
- Active vs Dormant Accounts

### Transactions
- Total Transactions & Total Transaction Volume
- Transaction Type Analysis (Transfer, Deposit, Withdrawal, Payment)
- Transaction Trends and Seasonality

### Loans
- Total Loans & Outstanding Loan Amount
- Average, Min, and Max Interest Rates
- Loan Type Performance (Home, Car, Personal, Education)
- Loan Aging and Maturity Analysis

### Cards
- Total Cards Issued
- Average Cards per Customer
- Active vs Expired Cards

### Customer Support
- Total Support Calls
- Resolution Rate (Resolved vs Unresolved)
- Issue Type Breakdown & Top Problem Areas
- Average Resolution Time
